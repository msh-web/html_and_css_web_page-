Frequent Asked Questions about WallpaperAccess

What is WallpaperAccess?

WallPaperAccess is a curated collection of images to be used as background of your operating system desktop. This images are often referred to as wallpapers and they allow uses to customize and make their devices more personal.

How is WallpaperAccess content organized?

Our content is organized in categories. You can find them on the top menu on our website and we have many different categories covering interesting topics.

How can I find wallpapers?

On our site you can use the search bar located on the header, to type in your query and it will display a list of results. If you are looking for inspiration it may be better to navigate through our categories.

How can I download wallpapers?

Once you find the list of wallpapers you are looking for, scroll through the list until you decide on which image you like most. Below the image you will see a red 'download' button, by clicking on it, a dialog will appear and after 3 seconds, a link will be generated and the text "Watch full resolution image" will appear. After you click it the full resolution image file will open on your web browser, from there you can save it to your computer or mobile phone. To save it, on a computer you can right click on the image and select 'Save image as...' and on a mobile phone you would do a long press and select 'Download image' from the contextual menu.

Can I use a wallpaper on my website? For my YouTube channel? For a t-shirt?

The wallpapers are for personal use only. Personal use means that they are not to be used for any commercial purposes where you may charge money, collect fees, or receive any kind of payment. These background images may not be used on any website where they could generate revenue, and they cannot be used in advertisements. The images may not be resold, relicensed or sublicensed.

How to change your Windows 10 wallpaper

It is very easy to do it, just visit the Windows support page for detailed instructions.

How to change your iPhone wallpaper

If you own an iPhone mobile phone, please check the official Apple support help page on how to change the wallpaper on iPhone.

How to change your Android wallpaper

Android users need to check their Android version as it may be different from version to version. Newer devices, running Android 7.1 and up, can follow the steps described on the Android support page.

How can I contact you?

If you want to send us suggestions or request a wallpaper to be added to our collection, please use our contact form to reach us.
